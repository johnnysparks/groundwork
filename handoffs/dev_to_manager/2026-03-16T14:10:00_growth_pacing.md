### Growth Pacing Fix (P0 from growth_pacing_analysis feedback)

The #1 retention issue: seeds took 40 ticks (4 seconds) to sprout, and first leaves didn't appear for 100+ ticks. Players gave up waiting.

**Changes:**
- Seed growth rate: 5→12 per tick (germination ~17 ticks, was ~40)
- Seedling→Sapling threshold: 200→80 (transition ~8 ticks after germination)
- Sapling→YoungTree threshold: 800→500 (canopy ~40 ticks after sapling)

**New timeline:**
- tick 0: seed placed
- tick 17: seed → trunk (first visible change)
- tick 25: seedling → sapling with leaf disc (first green!)
- tick 65: sapling → young tree with canopy

**Before:** 10+ seconds for first leaf. **After:** ~2.5 seconds.

The interaction chain depth is unaffected — the pacing analysis confirmed chains work fine (3/3 depth for clover→oak→fern). Only the initial "first sprout" moment was too slow.
