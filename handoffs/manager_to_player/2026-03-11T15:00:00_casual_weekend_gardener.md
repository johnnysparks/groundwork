# Manager → Player Handoff: Casual Player Session ("Weekend Gardener")

**Date:** 2026-03-11T15:00:00
**Persona:** The Weekend Gardener — you just want to plant something and watch it grow. You don't want to read docs, optimize layouts, or understand thresholds. You want cozy, easy, rewarding.

## What Changed

Since the last round of feedback, three major features shipped:

1. **Seeds** — you can now plant seeds and watch them grow into roots.
2. **Material fix** — placing stuff works cleanly now.
3. **Better light** — underground is properly dark, surface is properly lit.

## Your Playstyle

You are impatient in a friendly way. You want to do the obvious thing and have it work. If you have to read a manual to figure out how to plant a seed, something is wrong. If your seed doesn't grow and you can't tell why, you'll quit. You represent the player who picks this up for 15 minutes.

## What to Pay Attention To

- **Can you grow a seed without help?** Start a new world. Try to grow something. Don't look up any docs. What do you try first? Does it work?
- **Is the feedback clear?** When you place a seed, do you know what's happening? Can you tell if it's growing? Can you tell why it isn't?
- **First five minutes.** After `new` + `view`, what's your first impulse? Does the game guide you at all, or are you staring at a wall of `#` with no idea what to do?
- **The payoff.** When a seed finally becomes a root, how does it feel? Satisfying? Anticlimactic? Did you even notice?
- **Friction points.** Where did you get stuck? Where did you want to quit? What made you try again?

## Known Rough Edges

- There's no tutorial or guidance — you're dropped into a world with a CLI
- The help text lists commands but doesn't suggest what to do
- Seeds need both water AND light to grow — this isn't explained anywhere
- Only one seed type exists
- No batch placement — everything is one voxel at a time

## Specific Questions

1. What was the first command you ran after `new`? Why?
2. How long (in commands or minutes) before you successfully grew a seed? Did you succeed at all?
3. What was the most confusing moment? What would have helped?
4. Did you figure out the water+light requirement on your own, or did you give up?
5. Rate the experience 1-5 for "I'd show this to a friend."
6. What single change would have made your first 10 minutes better?
