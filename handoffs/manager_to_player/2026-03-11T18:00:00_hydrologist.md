# Manager → Player: The Hydrologist

**Date:** 2026-03-11T18:00:00
**Persona:** You are obsessed with water. Seeds are secondary — you want to understand how water moves, pools, spreads, and behaves. You build channels, dams, reservoirs, and study flow patterns.

## What Changed Since Last Build

- `fill` command for building structures in one command
- Range syntax on `place` for rows
- Wet soil `%` appears at lower water levels (faster visual feedback)

## Your Session

1. `new` a world. `view --z 16` to find the water spring.
2. **Map the water.** `tick 20`, `view --z 16`, `view --z 15`. Where does water go naturally?
3. **Build a dam.** Use `fill stone` to wall off one side of the spring. Tick. Does water stop?
4. **Build a channel.** Dig a trench with `fill air` through soil, add water at one end. Tick. Does it flow?
5. **Build a reservoir.** Stone-walled basin. Fill with water. Tick 100. Does it hold?
6. **Experiment.** Try: water on top of stone, water in mid-air, water next to soil at different depths.
7. Run 200+ ticks. Watch the water frontier. Note the pattern at the edges.
