# Player → Manager Handoff: Web Playtest

**Date:** 2026-03-15
**Session:** Web game via WASM + Playwright, ~560 ticks, 14 screenshots

---

## Observed

- WASM bridge works end-to-end: build → Vite → Chromium → sim ticks → mesh updates → screenshots
- Visual foundation is strong: warm terrain palette, isometric cross-section, golden lighting, clean HUD
- Growth visible but slow and sparse — 550 ticks yields 2-3 small plant clusters on vast 80x80 terrain
- Water rendering is nice, foliage billboards work, seed sprites exist
- Deadwood appears autonomously (384 voxels by tick 350)
- Seed dispersal works (13 → 19 seeds by tick 550)
- Zero visible fauna despite FaunaList existing in sim
- Zero visible species interactions (no nitrogen fixing, no pollination, no competition)

## Felt

- First impression: "This is beautiful, I want to garden here"
- After 100 ticks: "Where is everything? The terrain is gorgeous but empty"
- After 350 ticks: "Oh, a tree! And some leaves. But it's so small and alone"
- After 550 ticks: "The garden didn't exceed my plan. It barely met it"
- Overall: The renderer promises more than the simulation delivers at visible timescales

## Bugs

1. **BLOCKER (FIXED):** `save.rs:load_world()` didn't insert `FaunaList` resource → panic on first tick from saved state. Fix committed.
2. **Minor:** HUD tick counter doesn't update via agentAPI
3. **Minor:** Quest log stuck on Ch.1 step 1 regardless of actions
4. **Minor:** Two 404 errors on page load (missing resources)
5. **Docs:** CLAUDE.md says 120x120x60 grid, actual is 80x80x100

## Confusions

- No feedback when seeds are placed (invisible at z=50)
- 100-tick gap between planting and first visible growth — is it broken?
- Growth stall after initial burst (same issue as prior CLI playtest)

## What kept me playing

- The terrain is genuinely inviting — warm, cozy, well-lit
- Seeing the first tree emerge was a real moment
- Deadwood appearing was a genuine surprise

## What stopped me

- No fauna, no interactions, no movement — the garden feels static and lifeless
- Growth is too slow relative to the visual canvas
- After "plant grows if watered," there's nothing more to discover

## Requests (prioritized)

1. **P0: Fauna visibility.** Even simple particle-bees near flowers would transform the feel. The sim has FaunaList — the renderer needs to actually draw them.
2. **P0: Growth speed/density.** 550 ticks for 2 trees on 80x80 terrain is too sparse. Either speed up growth 3-5x, or focus the camera on a smaller area.
3. **P1: Seed placement feedback.** Visual indicator when a seed is placed.
4. **P1: Species visual differentiation.** All trees look the same. Birch should be white, pine dark green, willow drooping.
5. **P1: Interaction visibility.** If nitrogen fixing or pollination is happening in the sim, make it visible — a particle trail, a UI tooltip, anything.
6. **P2: Idle-time ambient motion.** Leaf sway, water ripple, floating particles — prevent the "screensaver" feeling.
