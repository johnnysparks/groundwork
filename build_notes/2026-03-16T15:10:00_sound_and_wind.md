**Date:** 2026-03-16T15:10:00

## What Changed

### Sprint 105: Weather-Driven Wind
- Foliage wind strength varies with weather: rain=0.7, drought=0.12, clear=0.35
- Smooth lerp (2% per frame) for natural transitions
- Added `getWindStrength()` to FoliageRenderer

### Sprint 106: Rain Ambient Audio
- Procedural rain patter sound: band-pass noise at 1800Hz with 2.5Hz modulation
- Fades in over 2s when Rain starts, fades out when it stops
- Volume 0.12 (quieter than water spring at 0.08)

### Sprint 107: Build Notes (Sprints 104-106)
- Session documentation

### Sprint 108: Ambient Fauna Sounds
- Every 8-20 seconds, a random fauna creature makes its sound
- Birds chirp (descending sine sweeps), bees/butterflies buzz
- Only plays when fauna are actually present in the garden

### Sprint 109: Growth Shimmer Sound
- `playGrowth()`: ascending sine sweep (300→600Hz) with shimmer overtone (900→1200Hz)
- Triggers when foliage count increases by 30+ voxels
- Cooldown of 15 ticks (~1.5s) to avoid rapid-fire
- Plant growth is now audible

## Audio Landscape Now
The garden has a rich ambient soundscape:
- **Always on:** Water spring trickle
- **During rain:** Rain patter layered on top of water
- **When fauna present:** Periodic bird chirps and bee buzzes (8-20s)
- **During growth:** Soft ascending shimmer sounds
- **Player actions:** Plant pat, water splash, dig scrunch
- **Milestones:** Warm chime
- **Fauna arrivals:** Bird calls, bee/butterfly buzz

All sounds are procedural (Web Audio API, no audio files).
