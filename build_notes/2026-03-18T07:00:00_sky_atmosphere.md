## What Changed

### Sprint 250: Procedural Sky Clouds
**`crates/groundwork-web/src/lighting/sky.ts`**
- FBM noise (4-octave value noise) generates soft cloud shapes in the sky dome
- Two blended noise layers: thick cumulus + thin wispy for depth
- Drift slowly with time (matching sky UV projection)
- `uCloudDensity` uniform: 0.35 clear, 0.75 rain, 0.12 drought
- Fade at night to let stars through
- Height-masked: soft near horizon, fade at zenith

**`crates/groundwork-web/src/main.ts`**
- Cloud density lerps toward weather-driven target (0.3 rate)

### Sprint 251: Cloud Shadow Ground Plane
**`crates/groundwork-web/src/rendering/cloudshadow.ts`** (NEW)
- Transparent ground plane at GROUND_LEVEL using same FBM noise as sky clouds
- Creates visible drifting shadow patches matching cloud positions
- Weather-driven darkness: heavier in rain, thin in drought
- Night-aware: shadows fade when sun goes down
- Edge-softened at plane boundaries

### Sprint 252: Post-Rain Rainbow
**`crates/groundwork-web/src/lighting/sky.ts`**
- `uRainbow` uniform: triggers at 1.0 when rain stops, decays over 30s
- Full spectral arc (violet→blue→green→yellow→orange→red) using bandPos mapping
- Positioned as a fixed arc in upper sky
- Only visible during daytime (faded by uNightAmount)

**`crates/groundwork-web/src/main.ts`**
- Rainbow activates on rain→clear weather transition

### Sprint 253: Dynamic Water Sky Reflection
**`crates/groundwork-web/src/rendering/water.ts`**
- Water fresnel sky reflection now blends with uDayTint instead of hardcoded blue
- Water reflects golden at golden hour, blue at noon, cool at night

### Sprint 254: Sun Disc
**`crates/groundwork-web/src/lighting/sky.ts`**
- Visible sun disc: tight bright core (0.9985 dot) + warm amber halo (0.96 dot)
- Tracks actual sun position via `uSunDir` uniform
- Fades when sun below horizon

**`crates/groundwork-web/src/lighting/daycycle.ts`**
- Computes normalized sun direction from elevation/azimuth and passes to sky shader

### Sprint 255: Moon Disc
**`crates/groundwork-web/src/lighting/sky.ts`**
- Cool silvery moon disc opposite the sun direction
- Crisp disc + soft blue-white glow
- Only visible at night (uNightAmount > 0.05)
- Moon always above horizon (min Y = 0.25)
