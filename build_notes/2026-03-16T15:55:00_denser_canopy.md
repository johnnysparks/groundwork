# Build Notes: Denser Canopy Branching (Sprint 114)

**Date:** 2026-03-16T15:55:00
**Sprint:** 114

## What Changed

### Problem
Trees looked like "tall sticks with green tops" — trunks dominated visually while canopy was sparse and concentrated at the crown tip. Root cause: only 4 branch stubs (cardinal directions) seeded the space colonization algorithm, and growth was capped at 10 new nodes per tick.

### Fix
1. **8 branch stubs** (tree.rs `init_skeleton`): Added diagonal directions (NE, NW, SE, SW) alongside the existing cardinal (N, S, E, W) stubs. This gives the space colonization algorithm more starting points to grow from, filling the crown envelope more evenly.

2. **15 branches per tick** (systems.rs `branch_growth`): Increased from 10 to 15 new branch nodes per tick. Combined with the 8 stubs, branches develop faster and fill more of the crown volume.

### Result
Canopy coverage is denser and more evenly distributed around the trunk. Branch structures extend in 8 directions instead of 4, reducing the "stick with hat" appearance. The improvement is visible in the hero and close-up screenshots — more lateral spread and interior fill.

## Files Modified
- `crates/groundwork-sim/src/tree.rs` — `stub_dirs` expanded from 4 to 8 entries
- `crates/groundwork-sim/src/systems.rs` — `branch_growth` cap raised from 10 to 15
