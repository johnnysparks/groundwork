# Build Notes: "Make the Garden Alive" Session (Sprints 97-106)

**Date:** 2026-03-16T15:00:00
**Sprints:** 97-106 (10 sprints)
**Theme:** Every sprint followed the principle "make the garden more alive, not more polished."

## Session Summary

### Water (Sprint 97)
- Dancing sun sparkles (cell-based glints that wink on/off)
- Shoreline foam at shallow edges
- Stronger color contrast vs sky
- Increased ripple strength

### Seeds (Sprint 98)
- Golden sparkle particles at seed positions (2-3 twinkle every 0.3s)
- Brighter seed voxel color for soil contrast

### Mobile Performance (Sprints 99-100)
- DPR clamped to 2, tilt-shift DOF disabled, bloom half-res on mobile
- Shadow map 1024 on mobile, antialiasing disabled
- Chunk remesh budgeted to 4/frame on mobile
- Mobile camera starts at 1.6x zoom

### Weather Visuals (Sprints 101-103)
- Rain particles: 800 soft droplets during Rain state
- Drought haze: fog lerps to warm amber
- Weather event messages in HUD feed
- Squirrel fauna arrival messages

### Weather Feel (Sprints 105-106)
- Weather-driven wind: gusty in rain (0.7), still in drought (0.12), gentle default (0.35)
- Rain ambient audio: procedural patter sound fades in/out with weather

## What the Garden Feels Like Now

The garden is a **multi-sensory living world**:
- **See** weather: rain drops fall, drought yellows the air, skies clear
- **Hear** weather: rain patter joins the spring trickle, silence in drought
- **Feel** weather: leaves sway hard in rain, go still in drought
- **Read** ecology: HUD announces fauna arrivals, weather changes, growth events
- **Notice** seeds: golden twinkles mark where life will begin
- **Enjoy** water: shimmer, foam, sparkles make the spring feel alive

The principle "idle time must be rewarding" is now much better served —
a player who watches sees weather change, hears the garden shift,
watches fauna arrive, sees seeds twinkle, notices growth particles.

## Architecture Notes
- All weather effects (rain particles, rain audio, wind strength, drought haze) sync from a single `getWeatherState()` call in the animate loop
- Mobile detection is a single `isMobile` boolean computed at init, threaded everywhere
- No sim changes in this session — all improvements are renderer/audio side
