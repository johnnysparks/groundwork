# Build Notes: Ambient Life + Nitrogen Visual (Sprints 118-120)

**Date:** 2026-03-16T16:10:00
**Sprints:** 118-120

## What Changed

### Sprint 118: Falling Leaves
20 leaf particles constantly drift from canopy height to ground, swaying side-to-side. Olive, spring green, golden, and amber colors. Only active when garden has 200+ plant voxels (trees exist). Gentle constant ambient motion.

### Sprint 119: Night Cricket Sounds
Procedural dual-cricket chorus fades in during golden hour / blue hour (same window as fireflies). Two slightly detuned crickets at 4200Hz and 3800Hz with offset FM tremolo rhythms create natural evening chirps. Volume 0.03 — barely audible background texture. Synced to day cycle via `setNightAmbient()`.

### Sprint 120: Nitrogen Handshake Visual
Green particle shimmer rising from ground near trees with groundcover. Detects Trunk voxels near ground-level Leaf voxels within 5-voxel radius (same logic as sim's nitrogen boost). When 3+ groundcover leaf voxels are found near a trunk, emits fresh green particles.

This makes the canonical "clover near oak = 1.5x growth" interaction **visible to the player**. The discovery arc: plant clover → notice green shimmer → deduce the boost. Directly serves Big Yes: "Does the Nitrogen Handshake work?"
