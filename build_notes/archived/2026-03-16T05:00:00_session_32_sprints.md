## Session Summary

32 sprints in one continuous session. The game transformed from a static Three.js voxel renderer into a complete, deployable ecological garden builder with a SimCity-style game loop.

## Complete Feature List

### Sim Interactions (4 ecological systems)
- **Nitrogen handshake**: groundcover within 5 voxels boosts tree growth 1.5x
- **Pollinator boost**: bees/butterflies increase seed nutrient levels
- **Bird Express**: birds carry seeds 10-20 voxels to random locations
- **Pioneer succession**: bare moist soil → moss → grass → wildflower (autonomous)

### Rendering
- Species foliage colors (12 distinct palettes)
- Species trunk/bark colors (birch=white, pine=dark, etc.)
- Species root colors (x-ray underground differentiation)
- Fauna glow halos with additive blending + wing flutter animation
- Ecology particles (pollination, nutrients, water absorption, decomposition)
- Day cycle: 4 distinct moods (dawn pink, noon bright, golden amber, blue hour cool)
- Post-processing warmth tuned (0.06→0.02) for day cycle color variety
- Data overlays: water/light/nutrient heat maps (V key to cycle)
- Pulsing blue spring highlight

### Game Loop (SimCity Pivot)
- **Zone-based tools**: click fills radius (seeds r=4, water r=3, shovel r=3)
- **Auto-tick ON** by default, 100ms interval
- **Speed control**: -/+ keys for 1x/2x/5x
- **Water budget**: tools cost water (seeds=15, water=20, shovel=5), spring replenishes 2/tick
- **Garden Score panel**: live stats (plants, fauna, species) + composite score
- **Score milestones**: 500 Sprout → 1000 Seedling Garden → 2000 Thriving Grove → 5000 Living Ecosystem → 10000 Master Gardener
- **Event feed**: ecology-teaching messages with fauna roles, succession narration, periodic tips
- **Zone-based quest challenges**: 5 chapters (Welcome → Plant → Diversify → Discover → Ecology)
- **Seed placement particle burst**: visual confirmation on every click
- **New Garden button**: reset sim/score/water for replay
- **Save/load**: auto-save to localStorage every 10s, restore on reload
- **Clear tool labels**: Dig/Plant/Water/Soil/Stone with water costs
