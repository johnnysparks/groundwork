## What was built

### SIM-06: Seed light attenuation fix
Seeds are now explicitly transparent to light propagation. Previously they fell through both match arms silently (no attenuation at all). Now they attenuate like air — minimal reduction after assignment, matching their physical transparency.

**File:** `systems.rs` light_propagation — added `Material::Seed` to the transparent materials match arm alongside `Material::Air`.

### CLI-12: Fill protection in WASM bridge
The WASM bridge `fill_tool` now skips seeds and roots when using the shovel (dig), matching CLI behavior. Non-shovel tools already skipped non-Air cells via `place_tool`. The shovel fill was the gap — it could destroy living plants in a region.

**File:** `wasm_bridge.rs` fill_tool — added `is_shovel` check that reads material before digging, skips Seed and Root.

### SIM-07: Nutrient cycling system
Added a complete nutrient cycle connecting soil composition, bacteria, roots, and plant growth:

1. **Nutrient generation** (soil_evolution): Bacteria decompose organic matter into plant-available nutrients. Rate scales with `bacteria × organic / 6400` per 10-tick cycle. Nutrients cap at `nutrient_capacity()` — rich soil holds more.

2. **Root nutrient absorption** (root_water_absorption): Roots absorb nutrients from adjacent soil cells alongside water. Transfer rate is 2/tick (slower than water at 4/tick). Root `nutrient_level` stores absorbed nutrients (visible in inspect).

3. **Seed growth boost** (seed_growth): Seeds now check adjacent soil `nutrient_level` (actual nutrients) instead of just `nutrient_capacity` (potential). Falls back to capacity when nutrient_level is 0 for backward compatibility.

**Cycle:** roots enrich soil with organic matter → bacteria decompose it → nutrients released into soil → roots absorb nutrients → plants grow → more roots → more organic matter.
